List of Statuses​

Chnaging based on field StatusID value

Initilizing​

Offline​

Chief cannot communicate with the unit It applies to node after passing configurable Minutes Until Offline with default value of 100ms

Idle​

The unit is in the dock and not moving (not necessarily locked as explained in the previous note).

Waiting for Clearance​

This applies for a unit that is paired with another one. Waiting for clearance indicates that the unit is waiting for confirmation from Chief that the paired unit is docked thus preventing the units running at the same time.
